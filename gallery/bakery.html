Our Bakery

Here at Shantel's Sweets, we cater to everyone with a sweet tooth. Our goods are guarnteed to be just what the doctor ordered.

ANGEL CAKE


We start our day at the crack of dawn to bake our own muffins, bread, and dinner rolls. Loaves not used that day are donated to the local food shelter.

Our Burgers


People come from all over to enjoy our lovingly made burgers. We grind our own locally-sourced organic beef and turkey so you know it's fresh and free from fillers and other nonsense. Go for one of our creative topping combos or stick with the classics.

Catch of the Day


Our chef works with local fisherman to pick the freshest the sea has to offer for our daily seafood special. Our Roast Cod Caponata with Roasted Potatoes is an old favorite with our regulars.

Fries


Our fries are the crispiest fries in the game. Made from locally sourced potatoes and sprinkled with kosher salt, no one can resist!

Mouth Watering Fried Chicken


Our delicious crispy fried chicken is a fan favorite! Our chicken is locally sourced from an open pasture farm, making sure our chicken is healthy and happy.

Tabouleh


Our tabouleh is perfect for any vegan out there! All our vegetables are GMO free, which guarentees an all natural delicious flavor!
