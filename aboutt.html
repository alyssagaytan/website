About Shantel's Sweets

Shantel is the master mind behind this beautiful bakery, she made this all happen. The recipies she uses to make her delicious sweets have been passed down from generation to generation. Now that she has this knowledge, she has decided to share it with the world. Shantel's Sweets was founded in 2008 in New York, New York where Shantel and her husband Fernando resisded. Shantel took her business from a small shop next to a deli, to a chain of succesful bakeries across the nation.
